深度解读 MACRO INTELLIGENCE
This meeting marks a structural policy paralysis: inflation at 3.3% bars rate cuts, while tariff-driven demand weakness and Middle East-driven energy shock bars rate hikes. The Committee is trapped. 这次会议标志着结构性政策僵局：通胀3.3%封死了降息空间，关税抑制需求、中东能源冲击又压缩了加息选项。Fed被锁死在区间内，等待外部冲击自我消化。
四次供给冲击叠加（疫情→俄乌→关税→伊朗油价），任何单一框架都无法准确定价Fed下一步——这是2026年最难做的宏观交易背景。 Four sequential supply shocks have destroyed the Fed's ability to rely on any single policy framework. This is the hardest macro environment for rate-path pricing since 2022.

宏观裁定 MACRO VERDICT
基准情景（45%）：四季度单次降息，前提是中东局势部分缓和、能源价格从峰值回落、核心CPI重归2.5%以下。短期内持有久期资产需要更高风险溢价补偿。 FOMC的政策僵局不会在3个月内打破——没有一个方向的票数能形成压倒性多数。下一个信息窗口是5月CPI数据（6月13日公布）和伊朗局势演变。 Base case (45%): one 25bp cut in Q4 2026, contingent on partial Middle East de-escalation and energy normalization. Duration risk premium is underpriced at current levels. Watch May CPI (June 13) and Iran ceasefire signals as the next binary catalysts.
